3-Point Perspective City

This piece is a 3-point perspective city made from my imagination. It was a drawing assignment in my Computer Animation and Web Design class. It was the final assignment in our mini unit about perspective. In the previous weeks, my class had learned about 1-point and 2-point perspective, and I was very excited to learn about 3-point perspective. I ended up really liking the piece I made. I thought the use of 3-point perspective helped to make the buildings look interesting and like they're towering high up into the sky as the viewer looks down on them. It was drawn traditionally using pencils on January 16th, 2022.
